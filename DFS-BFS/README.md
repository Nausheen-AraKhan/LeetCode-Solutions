🚀This folder contains LeetCode solutions of the problems based on DFS & BFS
DFS (Depth-First Search) and BFS (Breadth-First Search) are two common algorithms used to traverse or search through graphs and trees.
DFS (Depth-First Search)
DFS explores as far as possible along one branch before backtracking.
How it works:
Start at a node.
Visit one of its unvisited neighbors.
Keep going deeper until no more unvisited neighbors exist.
Backtrack and explore other branches.

BFS (Breadth-First Search)
BFS explores all nodes at the current level before moving to the next level.
How it works:
Start at a node.
Visit all its neighbors.
Then visit neighbors of those neighbors.
Continue level by level.
